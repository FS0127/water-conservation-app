Water-Saving Tips

Fix Household Leaks

Leaks waste over 10,000 gallons per home annually. Check for running toilets and dripping faucets regularly.

Upgrade Appliances

Switch to WaterSense-labeled fixtures and ENERGY STAR washing machines to cut water use by up to 60%.

Smarter Lawn Care

Water lawns early or late to reduce evaporation. Use mulch, drought-resistant plants, and rain barrels to conserve water.

Mind the Tap

Turn off taps while brushing your teeth or washing hands. This simple habit can save over 200 gallons of water every month.
