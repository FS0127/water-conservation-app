Water-Saving Tips

Fix Household Leaks

Leaks waste 10,000+ gallons per home yearly. Check for running toilets and dripping faucets.

Upgrade Appliances

Switch to WaterSense-labeled fixtures and ENERGY STAR washers to save up to 60% on water use.

Smarter Lawn Care

Water lawns early or late to reduce evaporation. Use mulch and rain barrels to conserve even more.

Mind the Tap

Turn off taps while brushing teeth or washing hands to save over 200 gallons per month.
